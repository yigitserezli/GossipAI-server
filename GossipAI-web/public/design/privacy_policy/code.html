Data Collection

We collect only what is strictly necessary to provide our enigmatic insights. This includes conversational prompts, metadata required for session continuity, and minimal account details.

We do not scrape your personal contacts, track your precise geolocation, or monitor your activity outside the Gossip AI application.

Your queries are processed through layers of obfuscation. The AI interprets patterns, not identities. Once a session concludes, specific identifiable conversational nodes are aggregated and anonymized.
